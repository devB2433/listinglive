## What You Can Do Right Now

**Why we built it this way:** Your time is scarce. Learning a new app or wrestling with export settings shouldn't get in the way of posting a listing video. We wanted "I have a photo" to turn into "I have a video" in the fewest possible steps—so you spend time selling, not editing.

**Turn a single photo into a short video.** Upload your listing image, pick a style (we've got templates for living room, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, and more), and hit generate. In minutes you get a clip you can drop into Instagram, your website, or your next client pitch. No green screen, no timeline, no export settings—just pick and go. *Your benefit: a shareable video in minutes, with zero learning curve.*

**Why we built it this way:** One listing often means several strong shots—living room, kitchen, bedroom. Forcing you to publish them as separate clips is noisy; stitching them by hand is a chore. We designed a single flow that turns "many photos" into "one story" so the property feels whole, not fragmented.

**String several photos into one video.** Got five shots from the same listing? Add them in order, choose how long each scene stays on screen, and we'll stitch them into one smooth video. Same simple flow: upload, style, generate. *Your benefit: one cohesive video that shows the full space without manual editing.*

**Why we built it this way:** Every video you share is a chance for viewers to remember *you*, not just the listing. If your face and contact info aren't on the clip, that chance is lost. We made logo, avatar, and a closing card first-class so every video doubles as a small billboard for your brand.

**Put your brand on every frame.** Add your logo so it sits in the corner where you want it. Add your face—a small circular avatar—so viewers know who's behind the listing. And for the finish? A **profile card** at the end with your name, contact info, and a line of your choice. *Your benefit: every clip reinforces who you are and how to reach you.*

**Why we built it this way:** Agents run at different volumes—a few listings a month vs. dozens. One fixed plan would either overcharge light users or cap heavy ones. We wanted flexible plans and top-up credits so you pay for what you use and scale when you need to.

**Choose how much you need.** We offer plans that fit different volumes: Basic, Pro, and Ultimate, plus add-on credit packs when you need a little extra. Sign up, get credits, create. When you run low, top up or upgrade—no surprises. *Your benefit: no overpaying, no hard ceiling—just a plan that fits your rhythm.*

**Why we built it this way:** The best growth comes from people you trust. Invite codes let you bring in colleagues or clients and keep everyone on the same tool—so your network gets better listings too, and you don't have to switch apps to collaborate.

**Invite your peers.** Early users get invite codes to share. Send a link, they sign up, and you both keep doing what you do—with better-looking listings. *Your benefit: share the tool with your circle and keep your workflow consistent.*
